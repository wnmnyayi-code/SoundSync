
SoundSync Revenue Model - South Africa Only

User Roles:
- Artist: Registered/Independent/Record Deal (SARS & SAMBRO required)
- Fan: Can attend events, follow artists, upgrade to creator.
- Merchant: Can sell sound tools, equipment, and merchandise.
- Influencer: Paid only for verified user purchases via referral.

Withdrawal Thresholds:
- Artists: R1000 minimum
- Influencers: R1000 minimum
- Merchants: R1000 minimum

Revenue Distribution (R100 spent):
- Artist: 60% (R60)
- Influencer: 10% (R10)
- SoundSync (Platform): 15% (R15)
- SARS (VAT): 15% (R15)

VAT Rate: 15%
Currency: ZAR (Rands)
Location Restriction: South Africa Only
