SoundSync – Your Full Concept
🧱 1. Core Concept

Platform to connect music artists, fans, promoters and merchants involved in music.

Music Rules:

Users can only use music they own (from phone storage).

App verifies ownership before upload.

During streaming:

Temporary instance of track created.

Deleted immediately after the session.

Prevents recording, downloading, or redistribution.

Fans can listen with screen off (background play).
