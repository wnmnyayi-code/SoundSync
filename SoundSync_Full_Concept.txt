
SoundSync – Full Concept and Functionality Overview

1. Core Concept
SoundSync is a South African-based music engagement app that connects creators (artists), fans, influencers, and merchants. 
The app allows creators to live-stream the music they own directly from their phone storage. Each live session creates a temporary instance of the track that is automatically deleted after the session. 
Fans can’t record or download this music.

2. User Types
Users can choose from four account types:
- Artist (creator)
- Fan
- Merchant
- Influencer

Subscription Levels:
- Basic: Access to one role (Artist, Fan, Merchant, or Influencer).
- Standard: Access to two roles.
- Premium: Full access to all four roles.

3. Artist Features
- Register as: independent artist, registered artist, or signed to a record deal.
- Disclose tax and financial info for audit upon request (with fee).
- Must provide SARS clearance certificate.
- Must declare SAMBRO registration for owned music.
- Option to register music via SoundSync with SAMBRO (fee applies).
- Must declare sampled music (in line with SA law).
- Upload high-resolution profile pictures.
- Create 1 playlist (10 tracks limit for trial).
- Schedule live listening parties.
- Fans can join live sessions but not record or download.
- Can sell or rent merchandise space within SoundSync store.
- Merchants pay 10% commission to SoundSync (VAT included).
- Can sell music, merchandise, or digital sound tools.
- Option to pay a fee for professional mixing and mastering for low-quality music.
- Withdrawals allowed from R1000 and up.

4. Fan Features
- Fans can buy SoundSync coins (R10 = 200 coins, R50 = 1000 coins).
- Use coins to tip artists or buy merchandise.
- Can interact with artists.
- Can be followed back by artists.
- Can request to become creators (pending admin approval).
- Can create playlists using licensed music for a fee (paid to artist via SAMBRO).

5. Influencer Features
- Register as influencer to promote artists.
- Paid by artists directly for promotional work (negotiated rate).
- Paid by SoundSync a percentage when their referred users spend money or use coins.
- Can only withdraw once their balance exceeds R1000.

6. Merchant Features
- Can sell digital or physical music-related products (samples, tools, plugins, software, etc.).
- No need to be an artist or fan to sell.
- Physical items have delivery radius limits (defined by seller).
- Digital items are downloadable only after purchase.

7. Payment and Revenue Model
- Stripe Checkout Integration for payments.
- VAT added to all transactions.
- Automatic financial audit tools available for admin only.
- Minimum withdrawal: R1000 for artists and influencers.

Revenue Split (Example: R100 spent by fan on artist, with influencer involved):
- Artist: 60% (R60)
- Influencer: 10% (R10)
- SoundSync Platform: 15% (R15)
- SARS (VAT): 15% (R15)

8. Admin Features
- Manage user verification (SARS, SAMBRO, etc.).
- View and audit all transactions (restricted access).
- Approve fan-to-creator requests.
- Manage platform-wide financial reports.

9. SoundSync Store
- Artists and merchants can list items for sale or rent (music gear, samples, etc.).
- SoundSync earns 10% commission on sales from permanent store ownership.
- Items include DJ gear, sound tools, plugins, and digital assets.
- Merchants and artists can set delivery radius for physical goods.

10. Technology Stack
- Framework: Next.js (App Router)
- Backend: Supabase (with Row-Level Security enabled)
- Authentication: Email + Google + Apple
- Payments: Stripe Checkout
- Hosting: Restricted to South Africa (geo-locked)

11. Additional Planned Tools
- Built-in mixing and mastering tool (for low-quality tracks).
- Real-time coin economy.
- Tax deductions and compliance with SARS.
- Influencer referral tracking and automatic payouts.
- Analytics dashboard for admin, artists, and influencers.
